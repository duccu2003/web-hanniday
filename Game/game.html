User manual:
- Move with Mouse
- Press the Left mouse or W key to use 1
- Press the Q key to use skill 2, and cools down for 15s
- Press F5 or Reload page to play again
- Press the arrow icon Back to the page to return to the character selection page
- "Bunnies" and "Carrot" are worth 1 point, while "Tokki" is worth 2 points
- You will WIN when you reach 1000 Points
